🏢 Business Analysis & Market Research
Saint Ann's School — NSW Market Feasibility Study
✓ Completed

A three-phase market entry feasibility study for Saint Ann's School's expansion into NSW, Australia. Using ABS demographic data, competitor mapping, and weighted scoring, the team narrowed 2,500+ suburban areas down to a single final location recommendation — with full evidence backing.

Role
Business Analyst in a 6-person team (G03) — led data analysis in Phase 3, contributed to scoring methodology and final client presentations.
Actions
Phase 1: Screened 2,500+ SA2 suburb areas using ABS demographic filters → shortlisted ~100 candidates.
Phase 2: Applied weighted scoring model to narrow to 3 clusters: Hills District, Upper North Shore, Lower North Shore.
Phase 3: Validated all 3 clusters across 5 dimensions — household income, competitor landscape, transport, political landscape, age distribution. Delivered 3 formal presentations + final recommendation report.
Results
Final recommendation: Lower North Shore — 8,639 households, 16.75% high-income bracket, zero bridge toll barrier, progressive community stance, excellent public transport. Backed by full quantitative and qualitative evidence across all 5 dimensions.
